Interschool Football competetion
Won second prize in interschool Football Competetion

Considered to be the oldest national school football tournament in India, the Subroto Cup is named after Indian Air Force Air Marshal Subroto Mukerjee. He was the first Indian Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Air Force. The Subroto Cup was introduced to promote and encourage the game of football at the grassroots level in the country. The prestigious inter-school football tournament has been held annually since 1960. Around 50 school teams participated in the inaugural edition. It has been played in two age groups, sub-Junior (under 14 years) and Junior (under 17 years) since 1998.

Interschool Science Project competition
Won second prize in interschool Science Project Competetion

A good science project for an interschool competition could be a research project on a current scientific topic, such as: Investigating the effects of pollution on a local ecosystem Examining the properties and uses of a new material or technology Analyzing the genetic makeup of a particular species or organism Studying the impact of climate change on a specific region or population It's important to choose a topic that you're passionate about and that can be researched in the time you have available. You should also consider the resources available to you, such as access to equipment or research facilities. It's also important to make sure that your project is original and not a copy of someone else's work.
